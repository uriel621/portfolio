About me

I'm a front-end web developer. I was born in California in the San Fernando Valley but later in 2008 I moved to Albuquerque New Mexico where I started with my education in the University of New Mexico and began my career in IT. My passion for all things related to web developing began when I stumbled across code academy website recommended by one of my friends and took their free courses in web development that they offer. Later in January 2016 I moved to Los Angeles CA with a goal in mind to expand my opportunities in web development. The more I developed, the more comfortable I was and saw myself doing this for a living. After a couple of meet up sessions with other web developers and discussions in the internet, I chose LearningFuze to further develop my skills focusing specifically on web development.

Fridge 2 Plate

An application that allows users to look for recipes based on ingredients they.

Fridge 2 Plate is a web application that returns recipes when user gives it ingredients. This helps the customer decide and choose what recipe they want to cook based on the recipes they got in return and also feature their own recipes on the front page. The user is anybody who is hungry and is in the mood for making a really good meal

Technologies: LAMP stack (Linux, Apache, MySQL and PHP), APIs (BrainTree, Google OAuth, Facebook OAuth), JavaScript, jQuery, AJAX, jQuery UI, Bootstrap CSS, HTML5, and CSS3.
